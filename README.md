# Duct Plate Die Design Project

## Project Overview
This project involves the design and development of a **sheet metal die for manufacturing duct plates** used in HVAC duct systems. The objective was to create a precise and reliable die capable of producing consistent sheet metal components while maintaining dimensional accuracy and production efficiency.

The die was designed considering manufacturing feasibility, material behavior, and production durability.

---

## Material
- **Workpiece Material:** Sheet Metal (Mild Steel)
- **Die Material:** Tool Steel
- **Manufacturing Process:** Sheet Metal Stamping / Press Operation

---

## Design Work
- 3D CAD modeling of the **die components** using SolidWorks
- Development of **punch and die profiles**
- Preparation of **2D manufacturing drawings**
- Accurate **dimensioning and tolerance control**
- Assembly design of die components including punch, die block, and guide system

---

## Manufacturing Considerations
- Designed for **press machine operations**
- Ensured **proper clearance between punch and die**
- Focused on **durability and wear resistance**
- Maintained **dimensional accuracy of produced duct plates**

---

## Deliverables
- Complete **3D die assembly model**
- Detailed **part drawings**
- Manufacturing-ready **technical drawings**
- Assembly layout for die installation

---

## Problems & Design Challenges
- **Clearance Calculation:** Determining correct punch-to-die clearance to avoid burrs and material tearing.
- **Tool Strength:** Ensuring punch and die components can withstand repeated press loads.
- **Alignment Accuracy:** Maintaining proper alignment between punch and die for accurate sheet metal cutting.
- **Wear Resistance:** Selecting proper die materials to reduce wear during continuous production.
- **Dimensional Precision:** Ensuring produced duct plates maintain consistent geometry.
